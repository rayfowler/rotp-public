The Ssslaura Conclave

The year is 973 EE...
As the last of the Ecodomes close over the disparate surface of Sssla, preparations have also completed to enable the settlement of neighboring worlds. 
Knowing that your people view the environment as an intricate puzzle begging to be solved, the Great Families realize how critical the presence of these mysteries are to maintaining stability within the Conclave. 
As the Ssslaura begin transformation into a space-faring society, they appoint you as the first central authority figure in the history of your civilization. With your newfound authority as the Prime Magister, you must ensure that your people are provided with a steady stream of planets to colonize and environments to bring to heel. 
You have often dwelt on what strange alien civilizations must await discovery in the depths of space, and what will happen when your people encounter them at some point in their exploration. 
But, no matter who or what you encounter, you are certain that they will eventually kneel before the superiority of Ssslauran science, and that it is the destiny of your people to free every world that lies within this galaxy their ecological imperfection no matter the cost...
