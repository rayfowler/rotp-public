The Meklonar Dominion
The year is 1081 PA...

The Meklonars' search for immortality has proven thus far to be a fruitless endeavor. Exhausting every possible means and avenue of research, the frustrated overseers of the Dominion turn to you, the Supreme Arbiter, in the hopes that you will be able to provide insight into the paths that remain available to them. 
During your commune with the wise and ancient entity known as the Mainframe, you learn that your people must begin a search of the numerous worlds beyond Meklon if they are ever to find the answers they seek. 
You return to the leaders of the Dominion with this clear directive, and with renewed vigor, they in turn begin the efforts to develop the means to expand their reach to the various planets that litter the galaxy. 
With the first ships ready to leave orbit and began the exploration of nearby worlds, your people have never been more determined to achieve their collective dream. The Meklonar will achieve immortality no matter the cost, and woe betide those who dare to oppose them...
