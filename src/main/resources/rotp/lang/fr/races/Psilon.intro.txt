The Psilon Republic
The year is RC 417...

The Republic stands ready at last to react to the Deep Echo after years of intense preparation. The Psilon High Council continues the effort to respond to the psionic emanation via Meditation, but on the eve of the first foray into space all debates have ceased in the gilded chamber of the Senate Hall. 
Everyone turns to the heavens to watch the departure of the fleet from Mentar and the beginning of the long-awaited exploration of the cosmos. 
As a new page in the history of the republic is about to be written, you dwell on the burden your station will soon force you to bear: you are the oldest of the High Councilors, and your wisdom and determination will be sought by every member of the Senate in this exciting and uncertain period. 
The knowledge that your people will gain from this expedition to the stars will dwarf all that your civilization has learned thus far, and at the end of the trail lies answers to a most tremendous mystery.
